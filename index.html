Welcome to Raman Vijayan Soccer

School & Noble Foundation

Noble Foundation (Formerly, Noble Football Academy) is a non-profit organization established in 2009 for the promotion of football in the state of Tamil Nadu. Established in 2009 by Mr. Raman Vijayan, Ex – Indian National Team Footballer and Professional Player to have played over 12 years in Top Football Clubs in India & friend Mr. Jeevanandam.

Raman Vijayan Soccer Schools is the most professional of the soccer academies in Tamilnadu training over 1200 grassroots footballers in a Full Time residential setup after academic sessions in schools across the state.
